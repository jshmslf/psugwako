About

This GWAKo is a General Weighted Average (GWA) calculator designed to help Pangasinan State University (PSU) students, specially those graduating students, estimate their GWA based on their academic records.

The system analyzes the grades directly from a PDF copy of your transcript or grade report, no need to manually input your subjects. It automatically computes your GWA while excluding non-academic subjects like PE and NSTP.
